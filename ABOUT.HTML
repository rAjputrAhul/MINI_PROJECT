LOST AND FOUND

This is an institution based LOST AND FOUND website for the students of Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology which will help them to report a lost/found item inside the campus and help each other find their lost things. Here, a user is given interface for registering a lost/found item by entering its details, listing, and searching its properties in a database with admin. This website is constructed with HTML, CSS and PHP. Students may often lose important things like documents, notebooks, ID-cards etc, and this website can be of great help to everyone. Firstly the users will register (if they are new to the website), else login and post about the lost or found item.
